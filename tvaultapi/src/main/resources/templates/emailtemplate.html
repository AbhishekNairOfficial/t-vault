Onboarding of services account has been completed successfully, and you will now want to activate the service account in T-Vault and assign permissions to other users who you want to view or reset this accounts password.
To do so please complete the following steps:

Activate Service Account:

  1. Login to T-Vault using your NT account.
  2. Navigate to the "MANAGE" tab - "AD Service Accounts"
  3. Select the "View/Edit" option corresponding to the service account you want to activate.
  4. Update any information you need to.
  5. Click the "Activate Service Account" link at the bottom right.

Assigning Permissions:

Once you are finished activating your service account you can assign permissions to other users or groups you want to have permission to view or reset the password for the account.
  1. Login to T-Vault using your NT account.
  2. Navigate to the "MANAGE" tab - "AD Service Accounts".
  3. Select the "View/Edit" option corresponding to the service account you want to manage.
  4. Select the "Grant Permission" button.
  5. Navigate to the "Groups" or "User" tab.
  6. Provide the group or user name you want to give permissions to and select either read, reset, or deny depending on the level of access you want to give.
Please contact Cloud Support if you need assistance.
